Must I upgrade to the Next Investment Plan?

No, you are free to invest in whatsoever plan in which you wish to invest in.

Are there withdrawal charges?

No, at TrioxTrade we do not incur any charges before and after withdrawal.

How many times can I invest in a certain plan?

You can invest in any certain plan for as much as you want.

Must I make reinvestments?

You do not necessarily need to reinvest immediately after your earnings has been dispatched to your wallet.

Can the synchronisation fee be deducted from my earnings?

No, we do not deduct the synchronisation fee from your earnings.

How many times do I get to pay the synchronisation fee?

The synchronisation fee is paid just once.

Can I make investments without having a bitcoin wallet?

No, in order to make an investment you need to have a correct wallet address (BITCOIN).

Can I receive my earnings directly to my bank account?

Earnings are only made to your wallet address provided during your account registration.
